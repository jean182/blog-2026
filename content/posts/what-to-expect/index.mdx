Welcome! This is my welcome post—though as you might notice, it's not my first time writing. I previously shared posts on Medium during my time as a software apprentice. I plan to migrate the best of those here, with a few modifications to improve the quality of the content.

As an aspiring software engineer, I'm still working on my writing skills. I plan to use this space to grow in that area, and I truly appreciate any **feedback** you might have.

## What to Expect Here

1.  **A Heartfelt Thanks:** I'm grateful you've found your way to this page. I hope you find something useful here. I'm planning to update this blog regularly.
2.  **Geeky Stuff & Technical Deep Dives:** This space is intended for "geeky stuff." I'm about to start my first job as a Ruby on Rails developer, so expect plenty of posts about RoR. I'll also share random things I enjoy, like movies, bands, video games, and maybe even food.
3.  **An Honest Evolution:** Since writing isn't yet my strongest suit, I’ll focus on keeping things interesting and enjoyable for you.
4.  **A Fresh Look:** I’m planning to add a custom logo for the header soon. If you’re a designer or skilled with Adobe tools and want to contribute, please  <a href="https://www.instagram.com/jeanm182/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">contact me</a>. As The Smiths said: <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w3qPMe_cCJk" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Please, please, please let me get what I want this time.</a>
5.  **Multi-language Support:** My first language is Spanish, and I believe it should have priority. Right now, my Medium posts are in English, but I plan to implement translations soon.

## The Story Behind This Blog

Initially, this project was just a way for me to learn **Gatsby**, which is an incredible library for building static sites. Over time, it evolved into a project I wanted to maintain and have fun with.

### The Hosting Stack
I chose [Netlify](https://www.netlify.com/) for hosting because it’s the easiest way to deploy and offers a great plugin for text editing—which is a lifesaver since writing everything in raw **Markdown** isn't always the most fun.

### The Domain Name
The URL might look familiar to some of you. It’s named after a <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1nDq1HoNm-E" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Green Day song</a>, and it carries a <a href="https://www.urbandictionary.com/define.php?term=nice%20guys%20finish%20last" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">significant meaning</a>. For me, the interpretation is a bit different, and perhaps I'll dive into that in a future post.

Regarding the extension, I originally wanted a **.io** domain, but it was a bit outside my budget at the time. I settled on **.dev** because I love programming, and it felt like a perfect fit. Plus, the **.com** version was being held for \$7k!

## Current Progress

I'll admit, I'm not writing unit tests just yet (don't judge, I'm getting there!), but I’d love to add a test coverage ribbon to the repo in the future.
